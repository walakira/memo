2.EDUCATION AND CAREER GUIDANCE
We provide career guidance and counselling to students that enable them to pick subjects and courses they are comfortable with and one that will lead to their  dream career.
People should be trained with an end in mind. We assist in providing all possible information and application assistance concerning the courses offered such as the length and admission requirement in the different learning institutions.
Personality and skill assessment
Different personaliyty tests are conducted to determine learners' strength and talents. Different career aptitude and assessment tests to ensure compatibility with interests, skills and values. Different personalities have different abilities.
Choosing a career and developing a career
We take learners through different sessions including talking to experts, watching career videos to ensure that they make informed decisions that have a destiny.This starts fron guidance on specific subject study area as early as primary level, and it's a continuous process.
Learners are guided on formulating career plans to be implemented continuously.Learners are also informed and or reminded on the various career pathways.
Talent identification and talent management
We guide learners on the general and specific study skills in the different learning area and at different levels. Such skills include; not taking, summary making, personal timetables, group work and discussions, keeping forcus on studies, preparing for and taking examination, tests and assignments, punctuality and time management
Internship placements

3.ORGANISATION DEVELOPMENT
We conduct the OD process to improve the organization's capacity to handle its internal and external functioning and relationships
Our intervens are then bases on the specific needs of the clients and our findings. We carry out a situational analyisis, review the existing systems, develop new systems and advise client organization on the time-bound implementation
 The organisatioal development process will generally involue the stages
Problem identification and definitionOrganisation development programme starts with the identification of the basic problems involved in the organisation. The awareness of the problem includes knowledge of the possible problems of the organisational growth, human satisfaction, organisational effectiveness, and use of human resources.
Having understood exactly what thhe problem is the, we can procees to collect the necessary data to slove the problem.
Collection of necessary dataData gathering is amongst our most important activites in the process of OD. We apply different techniques like, personal interviews, personal observations, and questionnaires to collect dat.
Different data and as such the question of which method to use depends upon the nature of problem the organisation eccounters. Having collected the necessary data, we proceed to analyse it
Diagnosis
Proper diagnosis will give correct identification of the problem and its causes and determine thescope of future course of action. in this the diagnosis involves a number of tehniques concerned with identifying issues, establishing priorities and translating them into aims and objectives.
WE apply specialized techniques of identifying the basic problems and issues Diagnosos assists us to locate the source of specific problem and see what changes are reqired in the system, the structure, ot in people
Planning of change and its implementation
After diagnosing the problem, we turn our attention to the planning of change and implementing it. OD intervebtion is considered to be the action phase in OD process. Intervention is a set planned, programmed activities, and techniques by which organisation and their clients collaborate in an OD program. The particular interventions to be used will depend on the target group in the organisation. One interventions, but selects the requirement.
But sometimes more than one intervention is used in a program.
Evaluation and feedback
As one stage ends and anther stage looms, evaluation is helpful to know as to what has been done, whether it is correctly done or not, and show wether further work is needed before proceeding to the next stage.
General institutional management
The services here include developing strategic plans and business plans, carrying out feasibility studies and policy development. Such services aim at improving the performance of our clients, primarily through organizatonal diagnosis and development of plans tailored towards the client's specific business improvement
Organizational design
We work with the Board and management of the client organizations to develop an efficient and effective organiztional structure.
Situational analysis and functional analysis are key stages in the process of organizational design
Development of systems and policies
We develop and review organizational systems and policies and develop manuaks. These polics and mabuals may range from board polices and board charter to human resources, finance and administration,operations ot programming
We offer management consultancy service for diverse sectors such as NGOs, private sectors, government, and parastatals
